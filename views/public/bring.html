bringing Reality U to your school


1. Schedule Reality U

  • Determine a date, location and the students you would like to participate in the event. We recommend the events for middle and high school grade students.
  • Each event session lasts a minimum of 75 minutes with no more than 75 students per session.
  • If the number of participants at your school will be under 200 students, you can ask an adjacent school/school district to join with you.

2. Contact Us

  • Email or call our administrative office regarding the event details in order to reserve your event: (404) 897-2970 or lmyles@cisgeorgia.org
